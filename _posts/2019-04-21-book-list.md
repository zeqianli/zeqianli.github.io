A list of books/lecture notes in statistical physics, condensed matter physics, biological physics, statistics, etc. Most are in graduate level. Undergraduate level texts are marked **[U]**. 


## Statistical physics, condensed matter physics
- Statistical mechanics
    * Mehran Kardar - Statistical Physics of Particles (2007)
    * Mehran Kardar - Statistical Physics of Fields (2007)
    * James Sethna - Statistical Mechanics: Entropy, Order Parameters and Complexity (2006)
    * Werner Krauth - Statistical Mechanics: Algorithms and Computations (2006)
    * R. K. Pathria, Paul D. Beale - Statistical Mechanics (3rd Edition, 2011)

- Renormalization Group, phase transitions
    * Nigel Goldenfeld - Lectures on Phase Transitions and Renormalization Group (1992)
    * Uwe Tauber - Critical Dynamics: A Field Theory Approach to Equilibrium and Non-Equilibrium Scaling Behavior (2014)
    * Subir Sachdev - Quantum Phase Transitions (2nd Edition, 2011)
    * John Cardy - Scaling and Renormalization in Statistical Physics (1996)
    * J. M. Yeomans - Statistical Mechanics of Phase Transition (1992)

- Condensed matter physics, field theory
    * R. Shankar - Quantum Field Theory and Condensed Matter: An Introduction (2017)
    * Alexei Tsvelik - Quantum Field Theory in Condensed Matter Physics (2007)
    * Anthony Duncan - The Conceptual Framework of Quantum Field Theory (2012)
    * Steven Weinberg - The Quantum theory of Fields (2005)
    * (Chinese textbook) 杨展如 - 量子统计物理学 (2007) (Zhan-Ru Yang - Quantum Statistical Physics (2007))

- Dynamical systems
    * Steven Strogatz - Nonlinear Dynamics and Chaos (2nd Edition, 2015) **[U]**
    * Stephen Wiggins - Introduction to Applied Nonlinear Dynamical Systems and Chaos

- Stat mech (misc)
    * (Chinese textbook) 朱海军 - 自旋玻璃与消息传递 (2015) (Hai-Jun Zhu - Spin Glass and Message Passing (2015))

- Math
    * Carl Bender, Steven Orszag - Advanced Mathematical Methods for Scientists and Engineers I: Asymptotic Methods and Perturbation Theory (2010)
    * George Arfken, Hans Weber, Frank Harris - Mathematical Methods for Physicists: A Comprehensive Guide (7th edition, 2012)
    * K. F. Riley, M. P. Hobson, S. J. Bence - Mathematical Methods for Physics and Engineering: A Comprehensive Guide (3rd Edition, 2006)

- General physics
    * R. Shankar - Principle of Quantum Mechanics (2nd Edition, 2011)


## Biology
* Uri Alon - An Introduction to Systems Biology: Design Principles of Biological Circuits (2nd Edition, 2019)
* William Bialek - Biophysics: Searching for Principles (2012)
* Mark Ptashne - A Genetic Switch: Phage Lambda Revisited (3rd Edition, 2004)


## Statistics, data analysis, machine learning 
- Statistics
    * T. W. Anderson - An Introduction to Multivariate Statistical Analysis (3rd Edition, 2004) **[U]**
    * Trevor Hastie - The Elements of Statistical Learning: Data Mining, Inference, and Prediction (2nd Edition, 2016)

- Information theory
    * David MacKay - Information Theory, Inference and Learning Algorithms (2003)

- Engineering
    * Martin Vetterli, Jelena Kovacevic - Foundations of Signal Processing (3rd Edition, 2014)
