A list online lectures in statistical physics, condensed matter physics, biological physics, statistics, machine learning, etc. 


## Statistical physics, condensed matter physics ## 
* [ ] ICTP - College of Spring College on the Physics of Complex Systems (ICTP)
  - [ ] [2019](http://indico.ictp.it/event/8644/) (*[Youtube Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLRwcSE2bmyBwzOuWthw_PCpyoYozJcJPu)*) 
    - Courses: Self-Organized Criticality, Electrostatic Interactions in Soft and Biological Matter, Statistical Mechanics of Two Dimensional Critical Curves, Randomness in Biology, Elements of Quantitative Finance
  - [x] [2018](http://indico.ictp.it/event/8299/) (*[Youtube Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLRwcSE2bmyBxsUP3FQdhP3J3Ema9vAxQB)*) (I went to [this one](/images/ictp-2018.jpg). ICTP was amazing.)
    - Courses: Nonequilibrium Behavior of Quantum Statistical Systems, Statistics of Extremes in Correlated Systems, Hierarchical Inference, Polymer Physics of Chromosome Folding, Reinforcement Learning
  - [ ] [2017](http://indico.ictp.it/event/7958/) (*[Youtube Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLYc-eBoIpXTIbSeZyUqEHPX6YbvEhHDrP)*) 
    - Courses: Optimization, inference and learning in biological systems, Random Matrices: Theory and Practice, Thermodynamics and out of equilibrium dynamics in disordered systems, Entanglement and complexity in many-body quantum physics, Quantitative Bacterial Physiology
  - [ ] [2016](http://indico.ictp.it/event/7644/overview)
    - Courses: Model-based inference in ecology and epidemiology (no video), Statistical mechanics of cellular motion (no video), Conformal field theory (no video), Non-equilibrium quantum systems (no video), Machine learning and physics of information processing
  - [2015](http://indico.ictp.it/event/a14244/) and [2014](http://indico.ictp.it/event/a13200/) weren't recorded. 

* [ ] ICTS - Bangalore School on Statistical Physics 
  - [ ] [2018](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL04QVxpjcnjj9xJA19Founo-zI_KnyPaF)
  - [ ] [2017](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL04QVxpjcnjjaJmtvrYJV_IGj9lVwOWho) 
  - [ICTS Youtube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/user/ICTStalks/)

* [Boulder School for Condensed Matter and Materials Physics](https://boulderschool.yale.edu/)
  - [Youtube Channel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC37kYds7QwN8w9vPBPkdVxA)


## Biology, biological physics ##
